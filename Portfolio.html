Projects

A sun-tracking solar panel system

A sun-tracking solar panel system optimizes solar energy collection by continuously adjusting its position to follow the sun's movement across the sky. It uses light sensors (LDRs) to detect sunlight direction and a microcontroller to process this data, sending signals to motors that rotate the panel. This alignment maximizes sunlight absorption, enhancing energy efficiency compared to fixed panels. Typically, it operates in either a single-axis or dual-axis setup for different tracking capabilities. This technology is especially useful for sustainable and efficient solar power generation.

School management system

A school management system is a digital platform designed to streamline various administrative and academic tasks within a school. It helps manage student records, attendance, schedules, and grades, making data easily accessible to teachers, students, and parents. The system typically includes modules for fee payment, communication, and event management, enhancing overall efficiency. It improves coordination among staff and enables quick decision-making through centralized data. This system supports schools in providing organized and effective education services.
